<h2>Punggol PC</h2>
<p>Running along Punggol reservoir and the Straits of Johor, this 6.1 km
water-themed park connector links Punggol Promenade to Gerald Drive. Completed
and opened to the public in 2010, it not only improves accessibility but
also enhances biodiversity in the area, featuring a variety of birds including
the majestic Grey Heron, migratory Little Egret and the commonly seen Collared
Kingfisher.</p>
<p>This park connector also joins Punggol Waterway and passes Anchorvale
Community Club, Sengkang Sports Complex and Singapore’s largest man-made
wetland, Sengkang Floating Wetland, before ending at Gerald Drive.</p>
