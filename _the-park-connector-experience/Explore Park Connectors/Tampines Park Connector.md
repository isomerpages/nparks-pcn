<h2>Tampines PC</h2>
<p>Linking Bedok Reservoir Park to Pasir Ris Park, Tampines Park Connector
runs along housing estates in Tampines Avenue 1 and 2, providing residents
convenient access to the nearby parks. It also allows users to travel to
Pasir Ris Town Park via Sungei Tampines, the natural habitat of water birds
such as the Common Kingfisher.</p>
<p>The Tampines Park Connector also runs under the viaduct of Tampines and
Simei MRT stations, allowing park users to travel to Simei estate.</p>
<p>
<br>
</p>
