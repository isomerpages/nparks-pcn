<h2>Hougang Avenue 3 PC</h2>
<p>The 4.4-kilometre Hougang Ave 3 Park Connector is the final piece to the
Central Urban Loop (CUL). This park connector connects residents at Hougang
and MacPherson to the North-Eastern Riverine Loop via the Serangoon Park
Connector, linking them to rustic green pockets such as Coney Island. The
Hougang Ave 3 Park Connector also provides Punggol residents with easier
access to the Eastern Coastal Loop via the Pelton Canal and Kallang Park
Connectors, with a cycling track passing Kallang Riverside Park.</p>
